Hiring: I am hiring fully-funded Ph.D. students (starting from 27Fall), RAs, and visiting students (e.g., CSC-funded). Prospective students please refer to Openings. Established in 2000, SMU now ranks No. 2 globally in Software Engineering in CSRanking (2020-2025).

I am a tenure-track Assistant Professor in Computer Science at Singapore Management University (SMU). Prior to that, I received my PhD degree from Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), advised by Michael R. Lyu. My research is on AI + software engineering, with an emphasis on improving the reliability and trustworthiness of software systems.

Featured research:
  • Software monitoring
    • Benchmarks for log analysis: AI platform for analyzing logs [LogPAI (IEEE Open Software Services Award)], Collections of log data [LogHub (127k downloads)], [LogHub-2.0 (ISSTA'24)].
    • Log management tools: Log parsing [ICSE'23; FSE'24; FSE'26a], Log compression [ICSE'26a], Configuration analysis [ICSE'26b]
    • Automatic logging assistants for developers: Benchmarking AI logging [TSE'24; TOSEM'25a], Logging quality [TOSEM'25b]
  • Agentic SE
    • Observabilty and reliability of agent systems [Arxiv'26a; Arxiv'26b]
    • Agent behavior analysis [ASE'25a]
  • Multimodal LLM for SE
    • Applications in web development: UI2Code [FSE'25; ASE'25b; FSE'26b]
    • A multimodal intelligence project on automatic webpage development: WebPAI
    • Evaluation platform & Leaderboard: UIBenchKit
